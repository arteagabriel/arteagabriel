About

Born in West Covina, CA, I am the youngest of five children. Growing up I was lucky enough to be surrounded by technology at home, mostly computers and video games. Technology had always fascinated me but I never looked into how everything worked until I started college. After a few years of community college, I transferred to California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. Cal Poly challenged my programming skills like never before, and now I know I have the knowledge and experience for any job. As of fall of 2016, I am a Cal Poly graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

When not programming, I spend much of my time listening to hip-hop, playing Street Fighter V, or reading my Kindle.
